Booking Information

For Students

Students can choose from a range of single, double, triple, or four-seater room options with both AC and Non-AC availability. Mess services include North Indian and South Indian food with flexible meal plans. Gym memberships are available for monthly, semester, and yearly terms.

For Faculty

Faculty members have access to premium room options and can avail mess and gym services. The hostel provides faculty-specific accommodation with privacy and comfort. Mess plans and gym memberships are fully customizable.

For Staff

Staff members can book rooms based on their specific requirements. Mess and gym services are available with flexible payment plans, ensuring affordability and convenience. Choose from AC or Non-AC rooms and enjoy a balanced diet with our mess options.
